In order to develop this project, Proyecto Esperanza would be contacting the Colombian justice department which is in charge of all the prison facilities in Colombia. Our judicial system has minimum security, middle security, and high security prisons. In this project we want to divide tasks in all three security level: Minimum security: most of this facilities are located in agreerian locations which would provide us the raw material (starch) to produce our bio resin Medium security: this facilities are location on middle sized cities and the power energy in this small cities tends to be cheaper so this means that we could provide equipment to produce the bio resin pellets and the film to produce the bag High security: this in my opinion is the most valuable of inmates population in Colombia most of this prisons are built and run by the government of the united states due to this fact they are the most organized and developed facilities and would be perfect for packing and distribution Families of the inmates, of all security levels, would be in charge of running the collection of used bags classification and logistics to take the used bags to be used as fertilizers in hills where the agricultural activity takes place. I am convinced that not only we can produce income for the inmates and their families, but also reduce the use of plastic products in Colombia.
