Proyecto Esperanza is a project created to spread awareness about the struggles that inmates and their families have to go through when trying to reintegrate into society, we intend to not only spread awareness but help the inmates and families to find their second opportunity. Proyecto Esperanza has partnered up with Greenpack an environmentally friendly packaging company, to be able to give job opportunities to the inmates and their families.
